Mission Statement:

We aim to ease the discomfort of conversations around female specific health issues in children and adolescents. Our goal is to create an environment which is friendly and knowledgeable in the issues our daughters face regarding their growing and changing reproductive systems.

From birth to adulthood, we address their physical and social needs to allow them to grow, learn and perform at their highest potential.

Why Us?

Pediatric Gynecologists are trained in Obstetrics and Gynecology however they have a special interest in the care of girls and teens.

So, what is it that sets us apart from any other Ob/Gyn with this interest?

At Little Women Pediatric & Adolescent Gynecology, all we see are girls from birth up through college. Therefore, when young women walk into our office, they know this is a place for them. We focus on the whole child, from their scholastic endeavors to their athletic, musical or artistic ventures and how their physical health affects their overall success.

Dr. Wright also had a strong desire to learn more about the care of girls with complex and sometimes rare medical concerns. Therefore, she spent an extra year in training to learn about how different complex medical concerns can impact reproductive development and health.

On the other side, she learned how puberty can make a life with these complex concerns even more challenging. Her number one focus is to help all girls with all their unique circumstances transition to a fulfilling and healthy adult life, reaching their highest potential.
